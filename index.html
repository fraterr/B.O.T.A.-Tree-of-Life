About B.O.T.A. Tree of Life

This interactive application is a study guide and contemplation tool dedicated to the Builders of the Adytum (B.O.T.A.) system of Hermetic Qabalah.

It maps the 10 Sephiroth (Emanations) and the 22 Paths of Wisdom, which correspond to the 22 letters of the Hebrew alphabet and the 22 Keys of the Tarot Major Arcana.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Tree: Click any node or path to reveal its esoteric metadata, Hebrew letters, and B.O.T.A. color attributions.
  • Tarot Integration: High-definition Major Arcana miniatures automatically rotated and aligned with their respective paths.
  • Three Pillars Isolation: Easily filter and highlight the Pillar of Severity (Left), Pillar of Mercy (Right), or Pillar of Mildness (Middle).
  • Dynamic Gematria: View step-by-step Hebrew gematria breakdowns and sums for all Sephiroth and path letter attributions.
  • Sefer Yetzirah Text: Access the traditional Book of Formation description for each Path of Wisdom.

Created as an open source study aid. Dedicated to the light of the Ageless Wisdom.
